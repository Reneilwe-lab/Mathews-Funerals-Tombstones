Waiting Period Information

1. There is no waiting period for accidental death.

2. There is a six (6) month waiting period for death due to natural causes.

3. There is a waiting period of twenty-four (24) months for suicide.

4. No additional waiting period shall apply after the initial six (6) month waiting period, in the event.
